Q: tempeh mold name
A: Rhizopus Oligosporus

Q: Tempeh percent protein
A: 19-20%

Q: what is a presscake?
A: the puck left over after oil extraction

Q: Indonesians usually cut tempeh in what direction?
A: diagonally

Q: Why do Indonesians cut tempeh diagonally?
A: Stronger slices

Q: Red miso ingredients
A: soybeans, rice, salt

Q: Coriander Garlic Tempeh Recipe
A:
- crush one clove garlic in mortar
- add 1/2 tsp of ground coriander
- add 1 tsp salt
- add mix to bowl of 1/2 cup water
- dip tempeh slices in water
- pat with paper towels

Q: Tempeh thickness in ziplock bags
A: 1/2 to 1 inch thick

Q: Why does my tempeh have black spots or patches?
A: too much oxygen, or incubated too long
