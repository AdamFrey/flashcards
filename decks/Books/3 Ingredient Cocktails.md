Q: Simple Syrup Recipe
A:
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup water
- Stir over medium heat until sugar is dissolved

Q: Rich Simple Syrup Recipe
A:
- 1 cup sugar
- ½ cup water
- Stir over medium heat until sugar is dissolved

Q: Honey Syrup Recipe
A:
- 1 cup honey
- 1 cup water
- Stir over medium heat until integrated

Q: Rich Honey Syrup Recipe
A:
- 1 cup honey
- ⅓ cup water
- Stir over medium heat until integrated
